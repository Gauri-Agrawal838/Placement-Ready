FAQ on HR Interview Questions

Question: What are the common questions asked in HR Interview?

Answer:- Most common questions asked in HR interviews include questions on:-

  • introduce yourself
  • about the company/profile
  • relocation/salary

Question: What type of questions are asked in HR Interview for freshers?

Answer:- Most common HR Interview Questions for freshers include:-

  1. Tell me about yourself
  2. Strengths and Weaknesses
  3. Challenges
  4. Short term/long term goals
  5. Why should I hire you?

Question: What should I say in an HR Interview?

Answer:- In any HR Interview you should always try and talk about:-

  • your strengths
  • your goals
  • your teamwork and leadership skills
  • how your goals align with that of the company’s goal.

Question: What is HR Round?

Answer:- HR Round is the final interview round. It can either be combined with Technical round or a seperate round on its own.

Question: How long does HR Round takes?

Answer:- HR Round duration totally depends on the company. Some company’s have their HR Round for 15-20 minutes while some can have an hour long HR Round.

Question: Is HR Round same for all companies?

Answer:- No. HR round is different for every company. In some companies the HR round is short lasting 10 minutes while some companies have a lengthy HR round. In some cases they can also have multiple HR rounds. Read about different HR rounds here: Interview Experience
